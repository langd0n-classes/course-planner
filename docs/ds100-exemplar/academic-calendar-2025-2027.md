# Academic Calendar 2025-2027

This file provides a consolidated academic calendar reference spanning
Summer 2025 through Spring 2027.
Source: https://www.bu.edu/reg/calendars/
Use the human-facing section for quick reading and the machine-facing section
for parsing and automation.

## Human Facing

### Summer 2025

- May 20: Classes Begin; first six-week session begins.
- May 26: Memorial Day Holiday, Classes Suspended.
- May 30: Substitute a Monday schedule of classes.
- June 19: Juneteenth Holiday, Classes Suspended.
- June 27: Summer first six-week session ends.
- June 30: Second six-week session begins.
- July 4: Independence Day Holiday, Classes Suspended.
- August 8: Last Day of Classes: second six-week session ends.
- August 25: Summer Degree Award Date.

### Fall 2025

- September 1: Labor Day Holiday, Classes Suspended.
- September 2: Classes Begin; First Seven-Week Session Begins.
- October 13: Indigenous People's Day Holiday, Classes Suspended.
- October 14: Substitute a Monday schedule of classes.
- October 17: First Seven-Week Session Ends.
- October 20: Second Seven-Week Session Begins.
- November 26 to November 30: Thanksgiving Recess.
- December 1: Classes Resume.
- December 10: Last Day of Classes; Second Seven-Week Session Ends.
- December 11 to December 14: Study Period.
- December 15: Final Exams Begin.
- December 19: Final Exams End.
- January 16: Fall Degree Award Date.

### Spring 2026

- January 19: MLK Jr. Day Holiday, Classes Suspended.
- January 20: Classes Begin; First Seven-Week Session Begins.
- February 16: Presidents' Day Holiday, Classes Suspended.
- February 17: Substitute a Monday schedule of classes.
- March 6: First Seven-Week Session Ends.
- March 7 to March 15: Spring Recess.
- March 16: Classes Resume; Second Seven-Week Session Begins.
- April 20: Patriots' Day Holiday, Classes Suspended.
- April 22: Substitute a Monday schedule of classes.
- April 30: Last Day of Classes; Second Seven-Week Session Ends.
- May 1 to May 3: Study Period.
- May 4: Final Exams Begin.
- May 8: Final Exams End.
- May 14 to May 17: Commencement Celebrations.
- May 17: All University Commencement; Spring Degree Award Date.
